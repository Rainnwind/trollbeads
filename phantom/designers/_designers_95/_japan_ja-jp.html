The Lithuania Group consists of six creative women. Coming from different backgrounds, today they are all working with making glass beads, taking their inspiration from art and their interest in nature.

When they produce glass beads they feel that this is a way to express thoughts and emotions. Each glass bead is eloquent, has its own history and personal meaning.
