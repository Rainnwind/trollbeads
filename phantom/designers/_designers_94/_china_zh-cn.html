To me, working with Trollbeads is very meditative. I create a sculpture or a small world in every bead. It pleases me to be able to dive in and work to the detail in a small scale in a world where we constantly receive lots of information and impressions. This is a unique chance for me and I appreciate it very much.

Anne Sofie Bodenhoff was born in 1980 and is a goldsmith, trained by Kim Buck. She finished her education in 2012 and won the Nordic Championship for goldsmiths that same year. Before Anne Sofie decided to follow her dream of being a goldsmith, she was an educated designer and worked in the textile industry for a couple of years.
