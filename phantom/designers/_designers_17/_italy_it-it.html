Ragnar R. Jørgensen was trained as a master goldsmith in the USA and Denmark, and has gained a unique experience from working with fine jewellery in Denmark, Sweden, Norway and the USA. Ragnar R. Jørgensen is today one of Denmark’s most renown jewellery designers, with a well established studio in Copenhagen.

As a result of his cosmopolitan background Ragnar R. Jørgensen merges the innovative Scandinavian design traditions with continental elegance and sophistication. This fine blend results in a distinct style of design that is recognizable throughout his work. Every line, curve and edge is placed with intent and confidence.

Behind every design there is a unique history and a higher purpose, although often hidden or unspoken.
